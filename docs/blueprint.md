# **App Name**: The Warm Crumb Bakery

## Core Features:

- Product Menu & Gallery: Browse a detailed menu of baked goods with enticing descriptions and high-quality images.
- Shopping Cart Management: Add, remove, and update quantities of desired items before checkout.
- Online Ordering & Secure Checkout: Securely complete online orders for pickup or local delivery, including payment processing.
- Bakery Information Display: View store hours, location details with map integration, and contact information.
- Customer Review Section: Read testimonials and reviews from other happy customers to build trust and inform choices.
- AI-Powered Pairing Tool: Receive intelligent suggestions for complementary baked goods or drinks based on selections.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep chocolate brown (#47301F) to evoke warmth, richness, and comfort.
- Background color: A very light, warm off-white (#F4F2F0) for a clean, inviting, and rustic canvas.
- Accent color: Soft peach (#F7D9C9) for calls to action, highlights, and a delicate touch of sweetness.
- Text color: Classic black (#000000) for high readability and a sophisticated contrast.
- Headline font: 'Literata' (serif) for a touch of classic elegance and vintage charm. Body font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for excellent readability and a friendly, modern feel.
- Use hand-drawn or line-art style icons related to baking and food, such as a whisk, croissant, or coffee cup, to enhance the rustic charm.
- Clean and spacious layout with ample whitespace, prominent product photography, and an intuitive navigation flow designed for ease of browsing and ordering.
- Subtle, smooth animations on hover for product cards and gentle transitions between pages to enhance the welcoming and premium feel.
